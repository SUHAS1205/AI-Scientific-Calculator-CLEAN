Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about CalcAI

How accurate is the voice recognition?

Our voice recognition system is powered by the Web Speech API and has been trained to understand mathematical terminology with 95%+ accuracy. It recognizes terms like "cosine", "integral", "derivative", and complex number expressions.

Is my calculation history secure?

Absolutely! All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. Your calculations are stored securely in a MySQL database with password-protected authentication via Werkzeug hashing.

Can I use CalcAI on my phone?

Yes! CalcAI is fully responsive and works seamlessly on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. The voice recognition feature works on mobile Chrome and Safari browsers.

Is CalcAI free to use?

Yes! CalcAI is completely free for students and educators. We believe advanced mathematical tools should be accessible to everyone. Premium features for teams and enterprises are coming soon.

What languages are supported?

Currently, CalcAI supports English voice commands. We're actively working on adding support for Spanish, French, German, and Hindi in the coming months.
